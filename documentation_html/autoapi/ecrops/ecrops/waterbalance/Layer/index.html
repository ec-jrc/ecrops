MFPTAB¶

Matric Flux Potential. The MFP is defined as the integral of the conductivity K(teta) from -Infinity to a certain teta (teta is the soil moisture content SM). The MPF is calculated as an integral of (K(teta) * 10^(pF) * eLog10) over the pF range considered.
